How to accept service

In order to accept someone's request, you should go to the menu for selecting available jobs.
To do this, move the cursor over the "Service" button in the upper left corner and click on the "Find service" button in the menu.



You will see a list of available jobs offered by other people, as well as see the price of the order.
By clicking on the work you like, you will see detailed information regarding this order.

If everything suits you, you can accept the order by clicking on the "Accept offer" button.

After accepting the order, you need to contact the customer of the work.
Information about the orders you have accepted can be found in the "Your orders" menu.
