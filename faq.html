How to request service

In order to get the service, first you need to decide what you want.

Then, if you don't know how to rate your proposed job, you can use an online calculator.
To do this, click on the "Calculator" button in the upper left corner.

In this menu, you must select all the options that fit your request, and then click "Calculate"

Thus, you will receive the cost based on your options. However, you can specify any cost when requesting the service.

The next step will be about how to make a service request
To do this, move the cursor over the "Service" button in the upper left corner and click on the "Request service" button in the menu.

Here you should indicate as clearly as possible what you want. If necessary, you can attach photos of what you need to fix or do. Then you set a price that you think is adequate for your work.
Finally, you click Submit.

After that, your request will be sent, and the person who will accept your order will contact you.
